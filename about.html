About Us

Words from Owner

At Chyaa Camp, we believe that a good cup of Chyaa can make your day better. Our Chyaa camp is a place where you can pause, relax, and savor the little moments.
Whether you're stopping by for your daily brew, catching up with friends, or enjoying a quiet moment of reflection, we're here to offer you a cozy, welcoming space to call your own.
What started as a small dream has blossomed into a community hub where every cup is brewed with care, and every customer is treated like family.
We’re passionate about serving high-quality Chyaa made from the finest tea leaf, and we take pride in offering fresh, delicious food that’s crafted with love.
Our mission is simple: to create a space where you can unwind, connect, and enjoy the simple pleasure of great Chyaa. We’re not just about what’s in the
cup we’re about creating a warm, friendly environment that feels like home.
Thank you for choosing to spend your time with us. Your support means the world, and we can’t wait to share many more cups of chyaa and great conversations with you. See you soon!
With gratitude,
Rishab Magar
Chyaa Camp,British Camp
